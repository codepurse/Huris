Organizational Assessment and Change Management

HURIS systematically analyzes and evaluates the performance of an organization; then together with its key leaders, identifies the strengths and weaknesses. We look into whether the organization is ready for change, has the capacity for it and helps the company ease into those changes.

Organizational restructuring

Organizations become hampered when their business models and directions have changed because of the internal and external influences affecting them. They need to adapt to survive and continue to grow or to speed up the growth they aspire for. HURIS conducts independent reviews and analyses of the different functions of their departments and study how business directions are best achieved.

Job Evaluation

HURIS employs various methods and approaches to assess the current value of specific jobs in an organization so that a satisfactory salary wage differential is determined. In a competitive and growing business environment, ensuring that employees are fairly compensated for their contributions and roles, and employers are able to attract and retain the best talents.

Job Description

Essential to the efficiency of each employee and to the recruitment process, jobs must be described accurately and correctly; responsibilities must be laid out for each function. HURIS assists this often complex and confusing process using agreed methods together with organizational leaders.

Competency Framework Development/ Assessment

It is not enough to know what a job entails, but it is essential to possess the knowledge, skills, attitude to do the job successfully. HURIS guides organizational leaders in defining these core competencies and understanding how they are manifested through both the organization’s and the individual employees’ behaviors.

Management developement and solutions

HURIS regularly runs public and in-house workshops and seminars for both private and government sectors. All are designed to answer the learning and development needs of HR executives and rising stars.

CESB Accredited Programs Managerial Leadership

Develop the discipline of Managerial Leadership by understanding the differences between leadership and management and then apply the right mix of their key skills to achieve positive results.

Strategic Decision Making

The assessment, analysis and management of complex situations are integral to sound decision making. In this program, participants learn to make recommendations that create strategic actions to meeting objectives.

Performance and Results Management

An effective performance management system calls for discipline. Improve your team's success rate. Learn the process from planning, managing and monitoring performance, and providing timely, precise and accurate feedback to them.

Building Powerful Teams

Develop your team's ability to define objectives and goals better, solve problems and resolve conflict, and foster trust in each one's contributions. Learn the various leadership and teamwork best practices.

Transformational Leadership

Become an effective leader. Learn how to help your team members’ map out attainable personal and professional goals, and create strategy to attain them.

Strategic and Critical Thinking

Strategic and critical thinking is an important skill that allows you to develop effective plans of action to attain organizational progress and change. This program provides you with the processes that build this essential leadership skill.

Driving Execution

A plan is only as good as the paper it’s written on when not properly implemented. This program will help you anticipate the challenges that arise and cope with the dynamics of change to achieve organizational change.

New Leadership Styles for 21st Century

Progressive times call for progressive leaders who are able to face the challenges of leading a team. Self-mastery and emotional intelligence are integral to being an effective leader. Learn skills in coaching, conflict resolution and handling difficult employees.

Thinking Out-Of-The-Box: Embracing Innovative Leadership

Be a creative leader. Learn to think out of the box and embrace innovation and creativity in the workplace to overcome obstacles, roadblocks and pitfalls in the workplace

Coaching: Raising Performance to the Next Level

Help individuals in your team boost their performance and become valuable contributors to your organization's quest for excellence and progress. Learn the process of coaching individuals for performance enhancement in this program.

Achieving a Positive Turn-around in Employee and Organizational Performance thru Positive Discipline

Acquire the skills needed to execute positive discipline to correct employee performance issues and help your team member achieve a positive turn-around. Learn and apply the concepts behind 'due process', and how to conduct a hearing or dialogue within the context of positive discipline.

Executive Coaching

More often than not senior executives find themselves on their own when it comes to making the final decisions, keeping the balance between the immediate, mundane matters and those strategic matters that really do matter. And then there’s holding oneself together in the face of frustration, pressure and urgency. HURIS provides coaching services for the decision makers at the top of the corporate ladder, as well as for those rising stars.

HR Business Partner Master Class Program (CIPD)

As part of HURIS’ partnership with UK-based Chartered Institute of Personnel and Development (CIPD), four public workshops are run annually under the aegis of CIPD accredited consultants. The series is a response of the international HR community’s need to move away from the traditional operational functions of the profession to taking more strategic, high level roles in any organization.

Participants may opt to take single classes or complete the course and earn a CIPD Certificate of Completion. These courses may also be run in-house for larger corporate groups.
